Word Wipe Online

Word Wipe a by Arkadium will help you expand your vocabulary and sharpen the mind. This word-finding game is a fan favorite. It starts out easy, but quickly picks up speed as you attempt to remove lines of letters tiles from the board within a time limit. This game is free to play online. You can find a level to keep you busy no matter what your day brings.

Word Wipe Game

Word Wipe is a game where you must clear all lines on the grid of 20x20 horizontally and vertically. You will initially only need to clear one line within 120 seconds. However, as the levels increase you will have to clear more lines in a shorter time. This word puzzle is easy to use. Open a new tab or window in your browser and access Word Wipe. Click on the beginning letter with your mouse. Drag your mouse to select an adjacent letter horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Then, continue with another adjacent letters. Drag your mouse to select each letter, then wipe away any remaining tiles. You will see more possibilities to find words by dropping the tiles that are above the word you have wiped. Play Word Wipe

Once the word game loads click the start button to begin. Words should not exceed three letters in length. By selecting adjacent letters, valid words will be apparent as the tiles change from yellow to green. The word will disappear when you release your mouse. Each letter can only be used one time, but you can move in any direction while the formation or your word is being formed.
